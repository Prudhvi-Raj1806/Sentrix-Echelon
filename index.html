Project Kavach

Forging the Future of National Disaster Response

Our Mission

An Atmanirbhar Shield for a Resilient India

The Challenge

In the face of a chemical disaster, our nation's first responders are our first and last line of defense. This critical gap in preparedness is a challenge to our national security.

Our Response

Answering the call from the Ministry of Home Affairs, Team Lumora is developing Project Kavach: a state-of-the-art, indigenously developed Virtual Reality (VR) Simulator for the elite personnel of the NDRF.
