Project Kavach

Forging the Future of National Disaster Response

A Smart India Hackathon 2025 Initiative by Team Lumora

Our Mission

An Atmanirbhar Shield for a Resilient India

The Challenge

In the face of a chemical disaster, our nation's first responders are our first and last line of defense. Yet, the very nature of these events - involving hazardous materials and unpredictable environments, makes realistic training nearly impossible. This critical gap in preparedness is a challenge to our national security.

Our Response

Answering the call from the Ministry of Home Affairs, Team Lumora is developing Project Kavach: a state-of-the-art, indigenously developed Virtual Reality (VR) Simulator. Our mission is to forge a new shield of preparedness for the elite personnel of the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF).

The Impact

We are contributing to the vision of an Atmanirbhar Bharat - a self-reliant India, in the critical sector of national safety. Project Kavach is the foundation for a new, scalable standard in disaster response training, designed to ensure that when disaster strikes, our guardians are prepared, our communities are protected, and our nation stands resilient.

The Kavach Simulator
